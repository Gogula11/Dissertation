# Appendix A: Ethics Approval

The ethics approval form (COMP5200M Ethics Checklist) was completed and submitted as part of the project proposal. The form confirmed that this project involves:

- No human participants
- No personally identifiable data
- No sensitive data
- No invasive procedures
- No deception

All data is synthetically generated. All software dependencies are open-source under permissive licenses. The project therefore qualified for ethics exemption without requiring full ethics committee review.

The approved form is attached to this dissertation as a separate document.
